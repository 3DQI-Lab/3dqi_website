The 3DQI Laboratory is looking for smart, motivated, high-energy people who are interested in medical imaging and its applications.

Research Fellow
We are currently accepting applications for 2~3 full-time postdoctoral research fellows with experience in medical imaging and machine learning for volumetric image analysis of tumors and organs. The candidate will also have opportunities to develop his/her research interests in the related areas.

Student Internship
We offer internship program for students to perform advanced research while furthering their education.

Visiting Researcher
We accept both visiting scholars (those who are research active academic staff at other institutions) and visiting doctoral researchers (who are registered as doctoral students at another institution) who are interested in medical imaging research and applications.

Others
