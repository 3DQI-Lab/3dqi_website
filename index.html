Welcome to the 3D Quantitative Image (3DQI) Laboratory in the Department of Radiology at Massachusetts General Hospital (MGH). Our laboratory is focusing on the translational research and development on advanced imaging analysis for precision diagnosis, evaluation of treatment response, and prediction of clinical outcome using techniques from the fields of Quantitative Imaging, Radiomics and Machine Learning. We collaborate closely with researchers from the Department of Radiology, Department of Oncology and Cancer Center.

With more than 10 years’ expertise in quantitative imaging analysis, we focus on developing advanced imaging and visualization technology to improve the patient experience and enhance clinical efficacy of 3D imaging.
